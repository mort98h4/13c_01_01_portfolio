Long story short

Since I was a child, I have always had an interest in drawing and being creative. As I grew up in the outskirts of Copenhagen, I developed an interest in Street Art and soon I would be painting on the walls of my childhood room. My parents thought that was a great idea...

So when they heard that I had applied to become a multimedia designer, they were of course relieved, and we all figured "Oh he is finally going to learn to do draw on his tablet. What a relief!". But only a couple of weeks into the first semester my priorities had changed - I simply love to code!
